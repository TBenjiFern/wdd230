Discover Moria

Location

Located in the southern end of the Misty Mountains, Moria is just the location for being close enough to wildlife to enjoy the fresh air of the wilderness whilst being positioned near one of the largest dwarven mines of Middle Earth. To the east of Moria, one will run into the Redhorn Pass, the Dimrill Dale, and Lorien where one, if they so desired to entered those sacred forests, could visit the wooden elves alongside their queen. To the west of Moria lies the secret pass into the mountain and a path leading north to Rivendell for those seaking refuge along a long journey. To the northeast of Moria lies your typical highway passes most travelers, unaware of the beauty of Moria, will take to cross the mountain. Paths such as the High Pass, Mountain Hall, and Mountain Pass alongside River Gladden are also potential roads and places one could visit if in a rush to pass the Misty Mountains. Our recommendation, however, is to stop for a spell at Morai where one can enjoy the scenery, the ale, the majest of dwarven mining, and where one might even strike it rich within the deeper quarters of the mines.

Economic Profile

Moria offers a large range of job and professions besides those related to mining, as one might mistakenly expect. Surrounding the entrances into the Mines of Moria, one can find numerous land surveying guilds which, upon request, will scout the Misty Mountains for ideal locations for travel, adventuring, and even setting up a home for those wanting to live closer to Moria. One will also find transportation services speeding the journey from Moria to other well known locations, such as Lorien, Rivendell, and even further beyond. Once inside the glories mines, there are, of course, mining parties which seek claims to various depths of the mines looking particularly for precious materials, such as mithril. On the higher levels of the mine, one can find blacksmiths, carpenters, traders, various locations for feasting, engineers of all types, and above all, our world renowned architectural and stone artists. Those seeking a future trade in stone working would be wise to consider coming to Moria.

Quality of Life

Residents of Moria enjoy a very comfortable lifestyle with all of the accessible facilities Moria has to offer. Boasting a generally high income rate for most locals in the dwarven capital, there is practically no lower class to be found in the halls of Moria. Our ever expanding mines allow for housing within the safety of the mountain, and an inner railway system insures fast transporation from the main trading and banquet halls straight to the work place. Even without the walls of the mountain, Moria stands as a marvel of a trading point which sees hundreds of travelers passing by each day, allowing for a wide variety of imports. 80% of visitors to Moria left an "excellent" review, indicated that they would gladly pass and stay in the Mines of Moria on their next visit. The other 20% found their visit pleasant and wished they had made more time to better enjoy Moria and the surrounding landscape.

Housing and Real Estate

As mentioned, housing and real estate is continually increasing above and below the Mines of Moria. Current residents find comfort, safety, and easy commute from the carved homes and mansions found under the Misty Mountain. Near to trading markets and the entertainment of the Halls of Moria, living inside the Misty Mountain is becoming a popular choice with newer residents. If you find the torch-lit grandeur of mines too stifling for you, then fear not! Multiple dwarven owned real estate companies have decided to collaborate with nearby rangers and elves to provide you expanded real estate options which span the entire range of the Misty Mountains. Contact any real estate agency within the Halls of Moria to find out which real estate guild is best for you. Most residents who have chosen to build their own homes exposed to the fresh air of the mountains have found that water, food, and the scenery is second to none!

Health and Wellness

In recent concerns about reduced exposure to sunlight and what effects that might have an individual health and wellness, The Chambers of Moria are proud to announce that committes dedicated to improving general fitness and well-being are being funded to solve all of your problems. New fitness and combat training locations are being carved into the local trading markets to provide easy access to supervised, professional workouts. Feel free to ride any one of Moria's newer vertical rail systems to arrive swiftly to the surface of the mountain in seconds from nearly any location within the mines. These vertical rail system allow long-term residents in Moria to quickly and inexpensively travel to the surface to feel the sun, breath fresh air, and better examine the beauty of the Misty Mountains. Collaboration with hobbit and elven services has also allowed us to improve interior comforts provided in our hand-carved housing in Moria. Never fear that you will have to sleep on stone again! You will sleep on the soften cloth and drink the finest ale available on Middle Earth.
