Target Audience

The target audience of the chambersofmoria.com web site is described below:

  • Who: Individuals looking for both business help and assistance, and those looking for partnership opportunities to help those in the community.
  • Age: 16 to 80.
  • Technology: Mobile devices, tablets, and personal computers.
  • Income: Lower income levels expected to use this site. Higher income organizations and individuals will be considered for partnerships.
  • Motivation: Seeking to support and encourage commercial and industral interests in the area.
