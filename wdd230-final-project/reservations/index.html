Room/Suite Types

Single Bed

Comfortable room for individual guests. Single Bed includes a bathroom, closet, and queen-sized bed for your stay. (King-sized beds available upon request)

Double Bed

Ideal room size for guests traveling in pairs. Double Bed includes a bathroom, closet, two queen-sized beds, and a television set.

Triple Bed

The Triple Bed room is the ideal room for a small family. This room includes a bathroom, two closets, two queen-sized beds, a television, and a futon/pull out bed.

Junior Suite

Much like the Standard Suite, the Junior Suite is intended for individual guests who want to travel in comfort. This suite includes a single king-sized bed, a closet, a bathroom, a television, and a kitchen area. Unlike the Standard Suite, the kitchen area and bedroom are not separated by a dividing wall, but the room size is expanded to provide comfort and ease-of-access.

Standard Suite

The Standard Suite is suited for guests intending to travel in comfort or for an extended period of time. This suite includes two king-sized beds, a television, two bathrooms, a kitchen area, a closet, and desk/eating space. The bedroom and kitchen area are separated by a dividing wall to improve privacy and comfort of life.

Bridal Suite

The Bridal Suite is equipped with all the accommodations a soon-to-be wedded bride could need. From getting your hair and makeup done to finding a place to relax before the big moment, the Bridal Suite is the ideal choice for getting ready for a wedding. This suite includes a fully stocked and expanded restroom, a king-sized bed, refreshment bar, stocked kitchen area, and gets special priority to room services. Additional accommodations available upon request.

Groom's Suite

The Groom's Suite is equipped with all the accommodations a groom will need. Much like the Bridal Suite, the Groom's Suite is equipped with a king-sized bed, refreshment bar, stocked kitchen area, expanded restroom, and an entertainment area for any guests the groom might expect. Grooms also get priority room service along with additional accommodations upon request.
