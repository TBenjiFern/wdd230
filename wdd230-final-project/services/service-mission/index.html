Service Mission Accommodations

We understand that not all of our guests are just visiting the temple. For our service missionaries, we offer the option of living in a suite to improve the quality of your extended stay near the temple. Suites, in contrast to regular rooms, have kitchens, supplies, entertainment, and more expanded/spaced living areas to make your stay feel as close to home as possible. In addition to your room, you will also receive priority access to room services, laundry services and dry cleaning, and extended access to our wifi. Please look at our selections below and choose the best option for your stay with us!

The Junior Suite is intended for individuals serving at the temple. This suite includes a single king-sized bed, a closet, a bathroom, a television, and a kitchen area. The kitchen area and bedroom are not separated by a dividing wall, but the room size is expanded to provide comfort and ease-of-access.

The Standard Suite is equipped to help you enjoy a long stay near the temple with a loved one or companion. This suite includes two king-sized beds, a television, two bathrooms, a kitchen area, a closet, and desk/eating space. The bedroom and kitchen area are separated by a dividing wall to improve privacy and comfort of life.

Questions?

Have questions about accommodations, suites, pricing, or other services we offer? Please contact us and share your comments or concerns through our Contact Us page. For abnormally long stays, please ask us about suite packages to lower your overall cost!
