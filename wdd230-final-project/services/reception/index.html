Wedding Reception Services

Getting married? To better serve our patrons and to improve your stay at our hotel, Temple Inn & Suites offers its wedding reception services to make your wedding day perfect! With our two Grand Reception Halls, you can make a requestion upon reservation to host your ideal reception near the temple of your choosing. In addition to our hall, take a look at our additional reception services available to you!

Catering

Each Temple Inn & Suites location offers professional level food catering services! After consulting with our wedding reception planner, we will help you build a menu which matches your reception theme and which will leave your guests happy and satisfied. Our services will prepare the food, setup a catering bar, and will attend and replenish food whilst serving your guests during the duration of the reception.

Setup Services

Worried about setting up the reception while also getting ready to be married? Temple Inn & Suites offers our patrons our wedding reception setup services to take care of all the heavy lifting for you. Request a time to meet with our wedding reception planner and personalize how you reception will be setup, and what theme you'd like your reception to follow.
